Our Story

Founded in 2015, Brew & Co. began as a small passion project focused on bringing exceptional coffee to Cape Town. What started as a tiny corner shop in Woodstock has grown into a beloved community gathering place with locations across South Africa.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around Africa and the world, with a special focus on supporting local South African growers. We roast in small batches and prepare each cup with precision and care. Our pastries are baked fresh daily using local, organic ingredients.

Our spaces are designed to be calm oases in busy cities—places where you can slow down, connect with others, or simply enjoy a moment of quiet with an exceptional cup of coffee.

Our Coffee Philosophy

At Brew & Co., we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage—it's an experience that connects people, cultures, and stories. Our approach to coffee is guided by three core principles:

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with farmers who share our commitment to sustainable and ethical practices. By establishing direct trade relationships, we ensure fair compensation for growers while securing the highest quality beans for our customers.

Artisanal Roasting

Our master roasters bring decades of experience to the craft, carefully developing unique roast profiles that highlight the distinctive characteristics of each bean variety. We roast in small batches to ensure peak freshness and flavor.

Precision Brewing

Every cup we serve is prepared with meticulous attention to detail—from water temperature and grind size to extraction time. Our baristas undergo extensive training to master the art and science of coffee preparation.

Community Focus

We believe coffee shops should be community hubs. That's why we design our spaces to foster connection and conversation. We regularly host events, workshops, and tastings that bring people together around their shared love of great coffee.

Our Journey

The story of Brew & Co. is one of passion and perseverance. What began as a dream shared by three friends—a barista, a roaster, and a chef—has evolved into one of South Africa's most beloved specialty coffee brands.

After years of traveling to coffee-growing regions around the world, studying under master roasters, and experimenting with brewing techniques, we opened our first location in Woodstock, Cape Town. The tiny shop, with just four tables and a small roaster in the back, quickly developed a loyal following.

Today, we operate five locations across South Africa, roast for dozens of wholesale partners, and offer barista training programs that have launched countless careers in the specialty coffee industry. Despite our growth, we remain committed to the values that guided us from the beginning: quality, community, and sustainability.
